# How enterprises are driving AI transformation with Claude

**Source:** https://claude.com/blog/driving-ai-transformation-with-claude  
**Published:** October 1, 2025  
**Categories:** Enterprise AI, Claude Enterprise

## Overview

Enterprise case studies showing how organizations achieve real business impact with Claude across pharmaceutical, automotive, cybersecurity, CRM, and financial services.

## Case Studies

### Novo Nordisk: Drug Documentation
NovoScribe platform (Claude on Amazon Bedrock + Claude Code + MongoDB Atlas):
- Clinical study reports (300 pages, ~2.3/year per writer) → **10+ weeks to 10 minutes** (90% reduction)
- Device verification: entire departments → single users
- Review cycles dropped 50%

### Cox Automotive: Car Buying
Integrated Claude across VinSolutions CRM, Autotrader PSX, Dealer.com:
- Consumer lead responses and test drive appointments **more than doubled**
- AI-generated vehicle listings: **80% positive feedback**
- Website content creation: weeks → same-day, 9,000+ client deliverables

### Palo Alto Networks: Secure Development
After evaluating multiple providers, chose Claude on Google Cloud Vertex AI:
- **20–30% increase** in feature development velocity
- Onboarding from months to weeks (2,500 developers enrolled)
- Junior developers completed integration tasks **70% faster**

### Salesforce: Autonomous Agents
Agentforce Agents powered by Claude through Einstein 1 Studio. Agents autonomously analyze customer data, execute transactions, and update records across platforms with safeguards (dynamic grounding, toxicity detection).

### IG Group: Global Operations
- Analytics teams save **70 hours weekly**
- **Productivity doubled** in certain use cases
- Triple-digit speed-to-market improvements for marketing
- Full ROI within three months

## Enterprise AI Success Characteristics

- **Concrete problem focus**: Target specific bottlenecks, not broad experimentation
- **People investment**: Training, champion networks, organic adoption
- **Measurable metrics**: Track productivity, time savings, quality improvements
- **Scale-first architecture**: Integration, security, compliance from day one
