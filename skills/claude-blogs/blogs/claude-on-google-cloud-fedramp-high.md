# Claude on Google Cloud's Vertex AI: FedRAMP High and IL2 Authorized

**Source:** https://claude.com/blog/claude-on-google-cloud-fedramp-high  
**Published:** April 2, 2025  
**Categories:** Product announcements, Claude Platform, Government

## Overview

Claude models are now authorized for FedRAMP High and IL-2 workloads through Google Cloud's Vertex AI, enabling federal civilian agencies and defense organizations to deploy Claude with strict regulatory compliance.

## Government Access

**FedRAMP High**: Federal agencies in healthcare, law enforcement, finance, and emergency services can handle sensitive unclassified data with Claude.

**IL2 (DoD Impact Level 2)**: Defense contractors and agencies can use Claude for non-controlled unclassified information, broadening defense use cases.

## What Federal Agencies Can Now Do

- Develop AI applications and workflows while maintaining compliance
- Access the complete Claude model family, including Claude 3.7 Sonnet
- Support digital transformation with advanced AI capabilities

## Managed Infrastructure

Claude on Vertex AI offers fully managed, serverless model APIs—no infrastructure provisioning required. The authorization is a step toward future IL5 compatibility for additional sensitive DoD operations.

## Implementation

1. Establish a Google Cloud environment with Assured Workloads configured for FedRAMP High or IL2
2. Access Claude models via the Vertex AI Model Garden
3. Build applications using Vertex AI API endpoints

Contact Anthropic's team for guidance on compliance integration.
