# Claude can now connect to your world

**Source:** https://claude.com/blog/integrations  
**Published:** May 1, 2025  
**Categories:** Product announcements, Claude apps

## Overview

Anthropic announced Integrations (remote MCP server connections), expanded Research with up to 45-minute investigations, and global web search availability for all paid users.

## Integrations

Built on MCP (launched November 2024), Integrations connect Claude with remote MCP servers across web and desktop applications.

**Initial launch supports**: Atlassian (Jira + Confluence), Zapier, Cloudflare, Intercom, Asana, Square, Sentry, PayPal, Linear, and Plaid. Stripe, GitLab, Box forthcoming.

Developers can create custom integrations in approximately 30 minutes.

**Example capabilities**:
- Zapier: access thousands of applications through pre-built workflows
- Jira + Confluence: product development and task management at scale
- Intercom: faster response to user feedback with bug tracking

## Advanced Research

Claude now conducts more comprehensive investigations across internal and external sources, requiring up to 45 minutes for complex inquiries. Breaks research requests into component parts, compiles detailed reports with source citations. Data access spans web search, Google Workspace, and any connected integrations.

## Availability

Integrations and advanced Research launched in beta on Max, Team, and Enterprise plans. Expanded to Pro on June 3, 2025. Web search available globally across all paid plans (all plans as of June 2025).
