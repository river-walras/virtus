# Artifacts are now generally available

**Source:** https://claude.com/blog/artifacts  
**Published:** August 27, 2024  
**Categories:** Product announcements

## Overview

Artifacts are now available to all Claude.ai users across Free, Pro, and Team plans, with support on iOS and Android. Since the June launch, users have created tens of millions of Artifacts.

## What Are Artifacts?

A dedicated workspace for viewing, iterating on, and building code, documents, and visualizations during conversations with Claude.

## Use Cases

- **Developers**: Create architecture diagrams from codebases
- **Product managers**: Build interactive prototypes for feature testing
- **Designers**: Develop visualizations for rapid prototyping
- **Marketers**: Design campaign dashboards with performance metrics
- **Sales teams**: Visualize sales pipelines with forecasting insights

## Sharing and Collaboration

- **Free and Pro users**: Publish and remix Artifacts with the broader community for collaborative global iteration
- **Team users**: Create Artifacts and share within Projects for secure teammate collaboration

## Availability

All Claude.ai users across Free, Pro, and Team plans. Mobile support on iOS and Android.
