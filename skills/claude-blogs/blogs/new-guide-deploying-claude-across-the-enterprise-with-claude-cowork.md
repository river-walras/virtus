## Overview

Anthropic introduced a guide for deploying Claude Cowork across business functions. The post positions Cowork as bringing Claude Code-style agentic capability to non-engineering teams such as analysts, lawyers, account executives, and marketers.

## Claude Cowork Capabilities

Claude Cowork works across local files and folders, connected apps such as Slack and Google Drive, and the browser. It can also work with Claude for Excel and Claude for PowerPoint, and can be extended with plugins, skills, and commands.

## What the Guide Covers

The guide includes:

- A five-level maturity model from chat Q&A to department-wide plugins
- Advice for choosing the first use case and structuring a pilot
- A six-month roadmap from champion teams to organization-wide deployment
- Examples from Anthropic finance, legal, sales, and product teams
- Customer stories from Thomson Reuters, Zapier, and Jamf
