# New connectors in Claude for everyday life

**Source:** https://claude.com/blog/connectors-for-everyday-life  
**Published:** April 23, 2026  
**Categories:** Product announcements, Claude apps

## Overview

Claude expanded connectors beyond work tools to everyday consumer services. New connectors include AllTrails, Audible, Booking.com, Instacart, Intuit Credit Karma, Intuit TurboTax, Resy, Spotify, StubHub, Taskrabbit, Thumbtack, Tripadvisor, Uber, Uber Eats, and Viator.

## Connector Growth

Since the Claude directory launched in July 2025, it has grown to more than 200 connectors across design, finance, productivity, health, and other categories.

## Dynamic Connector Suggestions

Claude can now suggest relevant connectors directly in a conversation. For example, it can surface AllTrails for hike recommendations or show multiple connector options when several apps could help.

## Privacy and Control

Anthropic states that Claude remains ad-free, with no paid placements or sponsored answers. Connected app data is not used to train models, the connected app does not see unrelated Claude conversations, and users can disconnect services at any time.

Claude can suggest or recommend actions, but it is designed to ask before booking or purchasing.

## Availability

Connectors are available on all plans, with mobile in beta, through the Claude connectors directory.
