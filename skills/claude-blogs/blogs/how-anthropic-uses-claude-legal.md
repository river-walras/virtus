# How Anthropic's Legal Team Cut Review Times from Days to Hours with Claude

**Source:** https://claude.com/blog/how-anthropic-uses-claude-legal  
**Published:** December 8, 2025  
**Categories:** Enterprise AI, Claude Enterprise

## Overview

Mark Pike, Associate General Counsel at Anthropic, shares how the legal department leverages Claude to streamline workflows without coding expertise.

## Four Workflows in Action

**Marketing Review Tool**: A Slack-integrated self-service system. Claude analyzes materials using a specialized skill containing historical guidance, flagging publicity rights concerns, unverified claims, and trademark requirements. Turnaround improved from **2-3 days to 24 hours**.

**Contract Redlining**: Compares document versions, highlights modifications, and recommends language from the commercial playbook. Integrated with Google Docs and Microsoft Office.

**Conflict-of-Interest Reviews**: Employees submit outside business activity requests through a form. Claude analyzes submissions against policy frameworks and sends recommendations to lawyers.

**Privacy Impact Assessments**: Using MCP servers to connect Claude with previous PIAs, lawyers generate new templates by referencing past documents.

## Implementation Best Practices

- **Start with pain points, not technology**: Identify draining, repetitive work
- **Use natural language**: "I just typed a normal sentence, describing what I wanted."
- **Maintain human oversight**: AI assists in drafting and first-pass review—not final judgment
- **Deploy Skills for consistency**: Package instructions for the team's voice, format preferences, and specialized expertise
- **Leverage MCP connections**: Link Claude to Google Drive, JIRA, Slack, and calendars
