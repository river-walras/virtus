# Deploying Claude across financial services

**Source:** https://claude.com/blog/deploying-claude-across-financial-services  
**Published:** May 5, 2026  
**Categories:** Enterprise AI, Claude Cowork, Claude Enterprise

## Overview

Anthropic introduced a financial-services deployment guide covering how firms use Claude across research, deal work, underwriting, claims, model reviews, and month-end close.

## Product Coverage

The guide explains when to use:

- Claude Chat and Research
- Claude Cowork for project-level work across files and apps
- Claude Code for quantitative and engineering teams
- Claude for Microsoft 365 add-ins across Excel, PowerPoint, Word, and Outlook
- Claude Platform and Claude Managed Agents for custom applications and agents

## Finance Agent Templates

Anthropic highlights 10 pre-built finance agent templates, including pitch builders, market researchers, KYC screeners, general ledger reconcilers, and month-end closers.

## Adoption Playbook

The guide presents a three-phase rollout: foundation, pilot, and scale. It is aimed at AI leaders deciding where Claude fits inside a firm and the engineers responsible for rollout.

## Customer Stories

Customer examples include AIG, Commonwealth Bank of Australia, IG Group, and Moody's.
