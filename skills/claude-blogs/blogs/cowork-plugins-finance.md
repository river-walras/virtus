# Cowork and Plugins for Finance

**Source:** https://claude.com/blog/cowork-plugins-finance  
**Published:** February 24, 2026  
**Categories:** Enterprise AI, Claude Enterprise

## Overview

Anthropic has announced significant updates to Cowork, expanding Claude's capabilities for financial services professionals with new plugins, cross-application workflows, and institutional data connectors.

## Cross-Application Workflows

Claude now operates across Excel and PowerPoint while maintaining context throughout multi-step tasks. Finance professionals can "analyze earnings, update a financial model, and build a summary slide – all without switching tools."

Available in research preview across Mac and Windows for all paid plans.

## New Anthropic-Developed Plugins

- **Financial Analysis**: Market research, competitive analysis, financial modeling, and presentation QA
- **Investment Banking**: Transaction document review, comparable company analyses, and pitch material preparation
- **Equity Research**: Earnings transcript parsing, model updates, and research note drafting
- **Private Equity**: Document review, financial data extraction, scenario modeling, and opportunity scoring
- **Wealth Management**: Portfolio analysis, drift identification, tax exposure assessment, and rebalancing recommendations

Available in the public GitHub repository.

## Partner Integrations (MCP Connectors)

- **FactSet**: Real-time market data, fundamental analysis, and earnings estimates
- **MSCI**: Index data, performance metrics, and constituent information
- **S&P Global**: Capital IQ Pro integration for company tearsheets
- **LSEG**: Market data and analytics for DCF modeling and portfolio rebalancing
