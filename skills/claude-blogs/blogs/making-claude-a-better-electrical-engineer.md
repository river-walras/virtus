# Making Claude a better electrical engineer

**Source:** https://claude.com/blog/making-claude-a-better-electrical-engineer  
**Published:** December 12, 2025  
**Categories:** Enterprise AI, Claude Enterprise

## Overview

Anthropic partnered with Diode Computers to enhance Claude's capabilities in auto-generating PCB reference designs using the Zener domain-specific language.

## The Challenge

Electrical engineers rely on extensive documentation to integrate circuit chips. "A typical chip might require up to a dozen ancillary parts." Claude's previous failures:
- Missing datasheet nuances about circuit configuration
- Misinterpreting schematic diagrams
- Misunderstanding Zener language syntax

## Solution Approach

Anthropic and Diode created custom testbenches that evaluated designs against higher-level requirements rather than rigid component specifications.

## Results

In blind evaluations with Diode engineers, Claude Sonnet 4.5's designs were preferred **8 out of 10 times** compared to earlier versions.

## Future Partnerships

This collaboration model is replicable across industries where Claude handles specialized, agentic tasks with measurable success criteria. Anthropic encourages similar partnerships through a formal application process.
