# What's new in Claude: Turning Claude into your thinking partner

**Source:** https://claude.com/blog/your-thinking-partner  
**Published:** November 20, 2025  
**Categories:** Product announcements, Claude apps, Work

## Overview

Anthropic has shipped numerous new features in Claude—memory, voice, file creation, and more—that together transform Claude into a thinking partner that stays with you through the messy process of thinking and building.

## Key Features

### Remember Your Conversations
Claude now keeps track of preferences, projects, and working style through memory, so context carries forward across conversations. Tell Claude you prefer prose over bullet points, or that you're planning a Q1 launch, and it will remember.

### Think Out Loud
Voice mode enables natural conversations with Claude on mobile for brainstorming, quick questions, or working through problems. Dictation available on both mobile and desktop.

### Work Wherever You Are
- **Mobile**: Draft messages, create calendar invites, make plans using location data
- **Browser**: Claude for Chrome handles forms and automates repetitive steps
- **Connected tools**: Notion, Canva, Slack, Linear, Figma—pulling in context and taking action directly

When ready to build, Claude can run analysis, create polished documents, spreadsheets, and presentations, or help prototype websites and interactive tools directly in conversations.
