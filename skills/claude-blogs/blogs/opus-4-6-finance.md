# Advancing finance with Claude Opus 4.6

**Source:** https://claude.com/blog/opus-4-6-finance  
**Published:** February 5, 2026  
**Categories:** Enterprise AI, Claude apps

## Overview

Claude Opus 4.6 represents a significant advancement for financial professionals, with a 23+ percentage point improvement over Claude Sonnet 4.5 on Anthropic's Real-World Finance evaluation (~50 investment and financial analysis scenarios).

## Key Improvements

### Research, Analysis, and Creation

- **Research**: Improvements on BrowseComp and DeepSearchQA enable extraction from dense, unstructured documents
- **Analysis**: 60.7% on Finance Agent benchmark (5.47% improvement); 76.0% on TaxEval
- **Creation**: Structured outputs like spreadsheets and presentations show significantly improved quality on first attempt

## Product Updates

**Cowork** (Research Preview): Desktop application enabling simultaneous analysis management with access to folders, file editing, and customizable plugins for corporate finance workflows.

**Claude in Excel**: Enhanced multitasking with support for pivot tables, chart modifications, conditional formatting, data validation, and auto-compaction.

**Claude in PowerPoint** (Research Preview Beta): Native slide building directly within PowerPoint, maintaining existing templates and design specifications.

## Industry Reception

Organizations including Hebbia and Shortcut AI report substantial productivity gains, with complex financial deliverables that previously required hours now achievable in minutes.
