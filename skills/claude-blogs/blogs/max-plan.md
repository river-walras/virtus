# Introducing the Max Plan

**Source:** https://claude.com/blog/max-plan  
**Published:** April 9, 2025  
**Categories:** Product announcements

## Overview

The Max plan is a new subscription tier for Claude's most active users, offering substantially expanded usage alongside priority access to upcoming features and models.

## Two Tiers

- **Expanded Usage ($100/month)**: Five times the Pro plan's allowance—suited for frequent Claude collaborators
- **Maximum Flexibility ($200/month)**: Twenty times Pro's capacity—for daily users relying on Claude across multiple tasks

## Target Users

Ideal for those who:
- Require extended conversations for iterative refinement
- Regularly process substantial documents and complex datasets
- Face time-sensitive deadlines
- Integrate Claude throughout their daily workflow

## Use Cases

- Workspace projects for writing, development, and analytical work
- Reflection and decision-making support
- Continuous engagement across multiple tasks

## Availability

Available immediately across all regions. Upgrade at claude.ai/upgrade.
