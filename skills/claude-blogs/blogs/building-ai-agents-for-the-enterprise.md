# Building AI agents for the enterprise

**Source:** https://claude.com/blog/building-ai-agents-for-the-enterprise  
**Published:** April 30, 2026  
**Categories:** Agents, Claude Cowork

## Overview

Anthropic introduces a guide for enterprise AI transformation and explains how organizations can use Claude Cowork to bring agentic workflows to teams without custom-building every agent.

## Key Themes

### Compounding enterprise advantage

The post argues that the enterprises pulling ahead are not only adding chatbots; they are embedding agentic AI into how work is done, how processes run, and what new product capabilities become possible.

### Three pillars of transformation

The guide focuses on:

- Upskilling employees with AI that reflects the organization's real work
- Compressing information-dense processes while keeping humans in the loop
- Building revenue-generating product capabilities, not only cost reductions

### Customer examples

The guide includes examples from L'Oreal, Lyft, and Rakuten, and covers how their AI deployments are being connected to business processes and institutional knowledge.

### Claude Cowork rollout

Anthropic frames Claude Cowork as a way to bring agentic capabilities to every team with a six-month deployment framework.

## When to Use This Blog

Use this source for enterprise AI transformation, Claude Cowork strategy, agentic adoption frameworks, and customer examples from L'Oreal, Lyft, and Rakuten.
