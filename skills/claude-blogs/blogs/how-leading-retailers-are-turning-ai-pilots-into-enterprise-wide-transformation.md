# How leading retailers are turning AI pilots into enterprise-wide transformation

**Source:** https://claude.com/blog/how-leading-retailers-are-turning-ai-pilots-into-enterprise-wide-transformation  
**Published:** January 28, 2026  
**Categories:** Enterprise AI, Claude Enterprise

## Overview

Most enterprise AI initiatives start with momentum but lose focus quickly. Organizations achieving measurable results follow a distinct playbook that transforms pilot programs into company-wide systems.

## Real-World Examples

**Shopify** deployed Claude through Sidekick, an AI assistant translating merchant questions into ShopifyQL queries without requiring technical expertise from users.

**L'Oréal** built a multi-agent system orchestrating 15+ specialized agents to transform questions into insights and visualizations for 44,000 employees across 150 countries.

**Lotte Homeshopping** launched a 24/7 AI assistant supporting partner suppliers through quality assurance inquiries, documentation validation, and regulatory guidance.

## The Path Forward

Leading organizations follow three essential steps:
1. Establishing governance and stakeholder alignment
2. Selecting low-risk pilot applications for initial deployment
3. Scaling proven solutions while developing organizational capability
